    What is the Hocking College Advantage?

    Hands on Learning

    At Hocking College, you’ll learn in an inclusive atmosphere that focuses on developing mind, body and spirit through world-class, hands-on experiential learning in a caring, supportive and nurturing environment.

    Affordable Degree Options

    We know you're not made of money which is why we believe that college should be affordable. At Hocking College, students can get a degree and career after just 2 years or transfer and save an average of $28,000 in student loans. 

    50+ Unique Programs

    At Hocking College, our students' interests are as diverse as they are. That is why we strive to have a selection of degrees and certificates that fit the needs of our traditional and non-traditional students.
